KINETIC MEMORY

Moving squares. Memorize the numbers. They vanish after 5 seconds. Click in ascending order while they dodge and block each other.

How to Play
• Watch the numbered squares bounce around
• Memorize which color/position matches each number
• After 5 seconds, numbers vanish — enter recall phase
• Click squares in order: 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6...
• Found squares keep moving and will block others
• 3 wrong clicks ends the game
