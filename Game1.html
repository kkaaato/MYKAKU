🎴 Shuffling Memory Match

Select Number of Players

How to Play:

  • Click cards to flip them and find matching color pairs
  • The Twist: After each failed guess, cards from the last 2 guesses shuffle positions!
  • Only the 4 cards from the last 2 guesses will move
  • Matched pairs disappear from the board
  • Keep track of the last 4 cards that moved!
  • Player with most matches wins!
